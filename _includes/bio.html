About Me

I am a postgraduate student pursuing the Masters of Machine learning and Computer Vision at the Australian National University. Currently, I am working on research problems in the fields of computer vision and reinforcement learning under the supervision of Prof. Liang Zheng.

Previously, I was working as a machine learning research engineer at Yahoo Japan, Tokyo. I received my Bachelors in Electrical Engineering with specialization in intelligent and cognitive systems from Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi (IIT Delhi).

My research interests include computer vision, deep reinforcement learning, vision and language navigation, biologically inspired (deep) learning algorithms, smart adversarial game-playing agents.
